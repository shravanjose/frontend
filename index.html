Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

This Classic Spaghetti Carbonara recipe is a traditional Italian pasta dish made with simple ingredients but packed with rich and creamy flavors. It's a quick and delicious meal perfect for any pasta lover.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, peeled and crushed
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Freshly chopped parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with a pinch of black pepper and set aside.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the crushed garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute, then remove the garlic.
  4. Turn off the heat and add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss well to combine.
  5. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture into the hot pasta, stirring continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
  6. Season with more black pepper and garnish with freshly chopped parsley, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
